Provide Fresh Food & Water Daily

keep your pet's bowl filled with clean water and feed them a balanced diet suitable for their breed and age.

Regular Vet Checkups

Schedule routine vet visits to ensure your pet is healthy and up-to-date on vaccinations.

Exercise & Play

Keep your pet physically active. Daily walks and playtime help maintain a healthy weight and reduce stress.

Love & Attention

Spend quality time with your pet. Positive interaction builds trust and strengthens your bond.

Proper Grooming

Brush your pet’s fur regularly, clip nails when needed, and bathe them as recommended.

Safe & Clean Living Space

Make sure your home is pet-safe — remove any toxic plants, wires, or small objects they might chew or swallow.

Training & Socialization

Train your pet with basic commands and expose them to people and other animals to avoid behavioral issues.

Identification & Microchipping

Keep a collar with an ID tag on your pet. Microchipping is also recommended in case they get lost
